Tips for getting the most out of grocery delivery

  • Order early in the day for the freshest pick of what just arrived from the souk.

  • Try the seasonal basket if you want a daily surprise of the market's best produce.
  • For bulk orders, message at least 24 hours ahead so the team can prepare accordingly.
